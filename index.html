Questions

Honest answers
to real questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

What is ATS and why does it matter?
+
An Applicant Tracking System automatically screens resumes before a recruiter reads them. Over 98% of large companies use one. If your resume is not formatted correctly, it can be rejected before any human sees it.
Do I need to know LaTeX?
+
Not at all. You fill in a structured form and our server handles all the LaTeX compilation automatically. You receive a finished, professional PDF without writing a single line of markup.
Is my personal information stored?
+
No. Your data is transmitted to the server, held in memory while the PDF compiles, then discarded. There is no user database. Nothing is retained after your session ends.
Why is there a short ad before the download?
+
CraftCV is completely free to use. The LaTeX compiler runs on a server with real costs. A single 30-second ad per download covers that cost. No subscription, no account, no other charges.
Which ATS systems have been tested?
+
Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, iCIMS, Taleo, BambooHR, and SmartRecruiters. Any platform that reads standard PDF text layers will parse CraftCV output correctly.
Can I update and re-download my resume?
+
Yes. Your last session data is stored locally in your browser. Return to the form, update what has changed, and generate a new PDF. The process is identical to the first time.
